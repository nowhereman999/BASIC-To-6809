{{DISPLAYTITLE:_glGetLightfv}}
The '''_glGetLightfv''' and '''_glGetLightiv''' statements return light source parameter values.


{{PageSyntax}}
: [[_glGetLightfv]] GLenum {{Parameter|light}}, GLenum {{Parameter|pname}}, GLfloat {{Parameter|*params}}
