{{DISPLAYTITLE:_glRotatef}}
The '''_glRotatef''' statement multiplies the current matrix by a rotation matrix.


{{PageSyntax}}
: [[_glRotatef]] GLfloat {{Parameter|angle}}, GLfloat {{Parameter|x}}, GLfloat {{Parameter|y}}, GLfloat {{Parameter|z}}
